# Privacy Policy

## Overview

This Chrome extension is designed to hide match scores and frame-related information on World Snooker Tour match pages to prevent spoilers for users.

The extension operates entirely locally in the user’s browser.

## Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

It does not use analytics, tracking tools, or external services.

## Website Content Access

The extension processes content from World Snooker Tour match pages solely to identify and visually hide potential spoiler information such as scores and frame data.

All processing occurs locally within the user’s browser. No website content is sent to any external server.

## Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage only to save the user’s preference (enabled/disabled state).

This preference is stored locally on the user’s device and is not shared or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if functionality changes. Any updates will be published in this repository.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository associated with this extension.
